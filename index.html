Our Mentors

King Shan Lui
Associate Professor - Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering, University of Hong Kong

Ms. Lui received the B.Eng. (First Class Honors) and M.Phil. (Distinction) degrees in Computer Science from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). After receiving her PhD degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, USA, she joined the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering of the University of Hong Kong. She has spent two summers in the Motorola Research Lab. in Boston. In 2007, she received the Cisco Research Initiative Award.

Wu Chuan
Associate Professor - Department of Computer Science, University of Hong Kong

Ms. Chuan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong, and currently serving as the Associate Head (on curriculum & development matters) of the department. She received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in the University of Toronto, Canada, and both her M.Engr. and B.Engr. degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Technology in Tsinghua University, China.
